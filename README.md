# AirBnB-Dataset-Visualization

# Executive Summary:

Using Tableau, I created interactive and visually compelling Tableau dashboards by integrating datasets with INNER JOIN and using filters to cut off unnecessary part of the dataset which improved the communication of data-driven insights to non-technical stakeholders. Since the major aim is to maximize profit through Airbnb, increasing the patronage of customers during summer and the end of the year period since there is generally a higher revenue during that period. Increasing the customers that patronize the 5 and 6 bedrooms apartments and also zipcodes such as 98134 and 98119 will also help to maximize profit for the AirBnB company since they had the highest average prices. To maximize profits through the above increase, I recommend that the marketing strategy team implement the following:

1. Targeted Advertisement such as using Social Media Advertisement to focus on reaching prospective customers during summer (June, July) and the end of the year period (December).
2. Also, prioritize the 5 and 6 bedrooms apartments and also zipcodes such as 98134 and 98119 since they have the highest average prices leading to a higher return on investment.
3. Send Discounts or other rewards or gifts or promo information via emails or texts for previous customers.
4. Better Client Relationships through quality on-site customer service. 

# Business Problem:

The general dream of any business is to maximize profit and expand as much as possible. After clearly representing data with clear visualization in Tableau, how do we make the most of these findings to observe trends that can be optimized to maximize the profit and also expand the business?

# Methodology:

1. Data Transformation with filters and inner join which improved dataset consistency.
2. Charts, labels and legends creating an interactive dashboard which enhanced visualization and communication of key data insights to stakeholders.

# Skills/Tools Used:

Tableau: Inner Join, filters, labels, legends, color, Charts.

# Results/Key Insights:

This is a detailed and visually appealing visualization that shows us:

-The most expensive zipcodes or the zipcodes with the highest average price. This makes it easier to consider preferred zipcodes based on budget or preference.

-The numbers of bedrooms with the most patronage and the total average price gotten for each. This shows us that the 6 bedroom type had the highest average price even though it had the lowest number of available units. The 6 bedroom room-type clearly had the highest charge and will be the best financial investment due to its high returns on investment.

-The summer period (June, July) and end of the year period (December) had the highest price or revenue for this particular year of our analysis. This makes a good point when considering when would be the best period to rent out for AirBnB to get even more profits and patronage.

This Visualization just emphasizes how the options in Tableau when well manoeuvred can pull out specific details that could be really useful while also being presented in a way that is easy on the eyes.
